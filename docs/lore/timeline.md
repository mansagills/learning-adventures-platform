# Spark Chronicles — Timeline

Chronological history of the world, oldest to newest. Append new entries in order; don't just tack new material onto the end without checking where it actually belongs.

### The Founder's Era (before Chapter 0)
The Academy is founded by a small group of teachers who wanted to build a school where every student's curiosity was taken seriously as a basic assumption, not an afterthought — see `factions/the-academy.md` for the founding philosophy. Around this same time, they first theorize that some students carry a "Spark" — a source of curiosity-driven power — though at this point it's an unconfirmed idea, not an established system. During this same ordinary, unremarkable era, **Jaylen's** Spark ignites earlier than anyone expected, over something small he got curious about, before anyone knew to watch for it (see `characters/jaylen.md`). Whether the Hush's earliest, quietest signs were already present — unnoticed — during this same era is left open for now; it's the thread the Season 1 finale is expected to pull on.

### Present day — Season 1 ("The Spark Chronicles")
New students enroll and discover their own Sparks. Jaylen, now established as the campus guide, notices the Hush's quiet drain spreading and begins investigating with the help of new students. See `SEASON_1_ARC.md` for the full chapter breakdown.
