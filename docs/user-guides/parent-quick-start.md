## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Visit the Registration Page
Navigate to the Learning Adventures website and click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"**.

### Step 2: Choose Registration Method
You can create an account using:
- **Email & Password** - Create a new account with your email
- **Google Sign-In** - Use your existing Google account
- **Apple Sign-In** - Use your Apple ID

### Step 3: Select Your Role
When prompted, select **"Parent"** as your role. This gives you access to:
- Parent Dashboard
- Child Account Management
- Progress Reports
- Parental Controls

### Step 4: Verify Your Email
Check your inbox for a verification email and click the confirmation link.

## Setting Up Child Profiles

### Why Child Profiles?
Child profiles are COPPA-compliant accounts that:
- Use anonymous usernames (no personal information)
- Have simple PIN-based login
- Track individual progress
- Allow parental oversight

### Creating a Child Profile

1. Log into your parent account
2. Navigate to **Parent Dashboard** > **Children**
3. Click **"Add Child"**
4. Fill in the required information:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Display Name | The name shown to your child (e.g., "Alex" or "SuperStar") |
| Username | Anonymous username for login (e.g., "adventurer123") |
| PIN | 4-digit code for child login |
| Grade Level | Helps recommend appropriate content |
| Avatar | Fun character icon (optional) |

5. Click **"Create Profile"**

### Managing Multiple Children
You can create up to **5 child profiles** per parent account. Each child has:
- Individual login credentials
- Separate progress tracking
- Personalized recommendations

## Navigating the Platform

### Parent Dashboard
Your central hub for managing your family's learning experience.

**Dashboard Sections:**
- **Overview** - Quick stats on all children's activity
- **Children** - Manage child profiles
- **Progress** - Detailed learning reports
- **Settings** - Account preferences

### Child Dashboard
What your child sees when logged in:

**Dashboard Sections:**
- **Continue Learning** - Resume in-progress adventures
- **Explore** - Browse all available content
- **Achievements** - View earned badges and rewards

## Accessing Games and Lessons

### Browsing the Catalog
1. Navigate to **Explore** or **Catalog**
2. Browse by **Subject**: Math, Science, English, History, Interdisciplinary
3. Filter by **Grade Level** or **Difficulty**
4. Click on any game or lesson to start

### Content Types

| Type | Description | Duration |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Games | Interactive learning with game mechanics | 10-25 mins |
| Lessons | Guided educational experiences | 15-30 mins |

### Featured Content
Look for the **Featured** badge on highly-recommended adventures that are perfect for getting started.

## Tracking Progress

### Viewing Progress Reports
1. Go to **Parent Dashboard** > **Progress**
2. Select the child whose progress you want to view
3. View detailed statistics:

**Available Metrics:**
- Adventures completed
- Time spent learning
- Skills developed
- Achievement badges earned
- Subject-specific progress

### Understanding Progress Indicators

| Icon | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Not Started | Adventure hasn't been attempted |
| In Progress | Adventure started but not completed |
| Completed | Adventure finished |

### Achievement System
Children earn badges for:
- Completing adventures
- Mastering skills
- Maintaining learning streaks
- Reaching milestones

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can my child play without me logging in?
Yes! Once you create a child profile, your child can log in independently using their username and PIN.

### How do I reset my child's PIN?
Go to **Parent Dashboard** > **Children** > Select child > **Edit Profile** > **Reset PIN**

### Is the content safe for children?
Yes! All content is designed for K-5 students and is reviewed for age-appropriateness. The platform is COPPA-compliant.

### Can I limit screen time?
Screen time controls are available in **Settings** > **Parental Controls**.

### How do I contact support?
Use the **Help** button in your dashboard or email support@learningadventures.com.
