# "Every Spark's Worth Following" — Channel Intro/Teaser (v2)

**Format:** Intro/cinematic, target ~40 seconds, 4 units (~10 sec each)
**Premise:** Same teaser concept as `intro-teaser.md` (the earlier Krikey-format draft), rebuilt here for **Showrunner** and **CapCut** side by side, now that the target platforms changed. Keep this version going forward; the original is Krikey-specific and can be treated as superseded unless Krikey comes back into the picture.
**Tone:** Warm, quick, playful — a teaser, not a full story.

**Reusable Jaylen descriptor (from `docs/lore/characters/jaylen.md`, still placeholder — swap before final production):**
*"Jaylen, a middle-school-aged kid, medium build, warm brown skin, short dark hair, wearing a Spark-orange hoodie over a plain tee, casual sneakers, loose easygoing energy."*

---

## Showrunner version

**Scene:** Town Square, cold open (all 4 units are one continuous Showrunner scene — same location throughout)

### Unit 1 — Cold open greeting (~10 sec)
**Beat:** Jaylen jogs backward a few steps, turns, waves at camera with a big grin, then gestures at the campus buildings around him.
**Showrunner prompt:**
```
Scene: Town Square, cold open
Beat: Jaylen jogs backward, turns to camera grinning — "Okay, so — welcome to the Academy."
Beat: Jaylen gestures at the campus buildings — "Every building's got its own games, its own challenges."
```
**Storyboard prompt:** Medium shot of Jaylen mid-turn, big open grin, one arm sweeping toward a row of colorful campus buildings behind him, bright daylight, clean 3D-cartoon style.

### Unit 2 — SPARK intro (~10 sec)
**Beat:** A small glowing icon blips into frame near Jaylen's shoulder; Jaylen explains what a Spark is.
**Showrunner prompt:**
```
Scene: Town Square, cold open
Beat: A small glowing SPARK icon blips in near Jaylen's shoulder; SPARK chirps "hi."
Beat: Jaylen, amused, explains — "Everybody here's got a Spark somewhere in them. It just means you got curious about something. Really curious."
```
**Storyboard prompt:** Close-up on Jaylen's shoulder, small glowing icon hovering beside him, Jaylen's expression warm and sincere, soft daylight.

### Unit 3 — Tone shift (~10 sec)
**Beat:** Jaylen's grin fades slightly, glances aside, mentions things have felt quiet lately; a hallway briefly grays out.
**Showrunner prompt:**
```
Scene: Town Square, cold open
Beat: Jaylen's grin fades, glances aside, tone quieter — "Lately though — some stuff's been a little... quiet."
Beat: Cut to a nearby hallway briefly losing its color, then snapping back to normal.
```
**Storyboard prompt:** Medium close-up of Jaylen looking slightly aside, soft concern on his face, cooler lighting than previous units; separate frame of a plain campus hallway mid-desaturation, muted gray tones fading back to color at the far end.

### Unit 4 — Hook (~10 sec)
**Beat:** Jaylen turns back to camera, grin returning with a knowing edge, points at the viewer, delivers the refrain.
**Showrunner prompt:**
```
Scene: Town Square, cold open
Beat: Jaylen turns fully to camera, grin returning, points directly at viewer — "Come find out with me. Every Spark's worth following."
```
**Storyboard prompt:** Medium shot, Jaylen facing camera dead-on, pointing finger toward viewer, warm confident grin with a hint of intrigue, bright daylight returning.

**Running estimate:** ~40 seconds across 4 units.
